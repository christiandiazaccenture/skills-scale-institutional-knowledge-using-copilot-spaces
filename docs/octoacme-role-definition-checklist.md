# Role Definition & Onboarding Checklist

Use this checklist to clarify project roles and ensure responsibilities are documented and understood during onboarding or process updates.

- [ ] Review core role descriptions in `octoacme-roles-and-personas.md`
- [ ] Document any project-specific role additions or modifications
- [ ] Define responsibilities, accountability areas, and key interactions for each role
- [ ] Confirm stakeholder alignment for new or updated roles
- [ ] Review with affected team members and update documentation as needed
- [ ] Add relevant roles to project management and release templates

---

_This checklist supports continuous improvement and clarity in team structure and operations._
